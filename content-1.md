**Setting Up Aspect Ratio**
--> 1. Go to **Settings**
--> 2. Then click on **Device** on the left navigation bar
--> 3. Click on **Displays**
--> 4. Change **Display Size** to *2194x1234*, if the screen are too small, set it to *1920x1080 (**Native**)*.
